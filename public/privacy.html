2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to provide our Service. We do not collect or store any personally identifiable information unless explicitly provided by you.

When you use our Service, we may automatically collect certain information about your device, including information about your web browser, IP address, time zone, and some of the cookies that are installed on your device.

2.2 Information You Provide

We do not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to use our Service. Any images you upload for conversion are processed locally in your browser and are not stored on our servers.

We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve our Service. We do not share, sell, or rent your information to any third parties.

4. Data Security

Your images are processed locally in your browser using WebAssembly technology. This means your images never leave your device and are never transmitted to our servers. We implement appropriate security measures to protect any information we may collect.

5. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our Service and hold certain information. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, if you do not accept cookies, you may not be able to use some portions of our Service.
